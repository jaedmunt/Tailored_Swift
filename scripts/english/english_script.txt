# Script for Voice Cloning

## Vowels:
- She sees the bee by the sea.
- Pick the thin pin from the tin.
- The bedspread is red.
- The cat sat on a flat mat.
- The car is parked far.
- The pot is hot.
- He took a good look.
- The blue moon is true.
- The cup was full of love.
- The bird chirped early.

## Diphthongs:
- They play by the bay every day.
- The boy enjoys making noise.
- How now, brown cow.
- She tries to buy the bright kite.
- The choice was quite clear.
- The loud crowd shouted out.

## Consonants:
- Bob brought a big box.
- Do dogs dig dens?
- Gary’s green grapes are gone.
- Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.
- Talking tigers take turns.
- Cool cats can climb cliffs.
- Fred’s friend found fresh fruit.
- Victor views various vehicles.
- The thick thorn pricked the thumb.
- They bathe with warm water.
- She sells sea shells by the seashore.
- Zebra zips through the zoo.
- Sheila shot the sheriff.
- Treasure is usually measured in pleasure.
- How happy is he who has health.
- Jack’s jokes just jolt Janet.
- My mom makes marvelous meals.
- Nine nannies need new nests.
- King Kong sings long songs.

## Combinations:
- The curious red fox jumped over the lazy brown dog.
- Quickly pack my box with five dozen liquor jugs.
- A wizard’s job is to vex chumps quickly in fog.
- Bright vixens jump; dozy fowl quack.
- Jinxed wizards pluck ivy from the big quilt.
